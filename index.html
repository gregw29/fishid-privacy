Privacy Policy for FishID

Last updated: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FishID ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our mobile application. By using FishID, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Photos

When you take a photo of a fish for identification, the photo is temporarily transmitted to our server for AI-powered species identification. The photo is processed in real time and is not permanently stored on our server. A copy of the photo is saved locally on your device in your catch log for your personal reference.

Location Data

With your permission, the app uses your device's GPS location for the following features:

  • Showing local weather conditions
  • Detecting nearby Marine Protected Areas (Legal Shield feature)
  • Calculating distances to nearby lakes and fishing spots

Your location is processed on your device and through weather/mapping services. We do not sell or share your precise location with third-party advertisers.

Account Information

If you choose to sign in, we use Google Sign-In via Firebase Authentication. This allows us to receive your name, email address, and a unique user ID from Google. Signing in is optional — most features of FishID work without an account.

Advertising Identifiers

Free users see ads provided by Google AdMob (rewarded and interstitial ads). AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID and related data to serve ads. Users with an active Pro subscription do not see ads.

Subscription Information

Subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide fish identification through AI image processing
  • To show relevant weather, location-based features, and nearby fishing spots
  • To maintain your account and sync preferences (if signed in)
  • To serve ads to free-tier users
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions

3. Third-Party Services

FishID uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — for fish species identification from photos
  • Google Firebase Authentication — for optional sign-in
  • Google AdMob — for advertising (free users only)
  • Google Play Billing — for subscription processing
  • Weather data providers — for local weather conditions

4. Data Retention

  • Photos sent to our server are processed and discarded; they are not stored.
  • Your catch log and photos saved locally remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.
  • Account information remains until you request deletion.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke camera or location permissions at any time in your device settings
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below
  • Delete locally stored photos and catch logs from within the app
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through Google Play

6. Children's Privacy

FishID is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit (HTTPS) and rely on Google Play and Firebase's security infrastructure for authentication and payments.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
